Privacy Policy

Yueni ("I", "my", or "me") is a JLPT grammar study application for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use

I do not collect, sell, or store personal information from users of the App. The App does not require an account, sign-in, email address, or other personal details.

Study Progress

The App may save your study progress, such as viewed grammar cards and memorization status, locally on your device. This information is used only to provide the App's study features and is not sent to me.

Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob to show banner advertisements. Google and its partners may collect and use information from your device to provide, limit, measure, and improve advertisements. This may include device identifiers, advertising data, approximate location derived from your IP address, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and similar information handled by the Google Mobile Ads SDK.

For more information about how Google uses data, please review the Google Privacy Policy and Google's information about advertising technologies.

Third-Party Services

Aside from Google AdMob for advertisement presentation, the App does not use third-party services to collect or share user information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Data Security

I do not operate a server that stores your personal information for this App. Information handled by Google AdMob is governed by Google's privacy and security practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will be effective when posted.

Contact Me

If you have questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me through the support contact provided on the App Store listing.

This Privacy Policy was last updated on June 29, 2026.

Yueni is designed to keep your study activity private while supporting the App through banner advertising.
